Long Haul Services

Our long-haul services guarantee that your goods will arrive at their destination on time and in the best of shape. No matter how long the haul may be, we will get it there safely.

We offer a variety of services that will keep you informed of every movement we make while hauling your load. These include direct communication, full security, and track and trace technology.

We will be happy to work with you to find out just what mode of a commercial truck will work best with your specific load. By doing so, we can save you a great deal of time, energy, and money.

D&D United Corp makes it a priority to purchase only the best quality trailers for shipping goods in. Each of our trailers is full sized for the purpose, temperature controlled, and completely secure. Our drivers are experts at transporting all manner of goods, ranging from heavy cable to cinder blocks. No matter what the cargo may be, our staff can easily handle the assignment.

Our staff of drivers and shipping experts are second to none when it comes to attention to detail. We keep a laser focus on the job at h and while also doing our utmost to keep you squarely in the picture. We guarantee a dry van shipping experience that is truly second to none.
